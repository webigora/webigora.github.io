About

DOVA, located at 229 Carlton Street, is a Sicilian restaurant by Chef Roberto Marotta and partners. (Our sister establishment, ARDO, has become a mainstay in Corktown.) We pay homage to Sicily’s exciting terroir and culinary traditions—but we also traverse other regions in the Mediterranean basin. Handmade artisanal pastas, fresh-caught fish and ethically sourced meats are the tipping point for our cuisine.

Our restaurant features an intimate private cellar, a lush main floor dining space and a hidden patio oasis. We can accommodate any style of celebration—from intimate dinners to cocktail receptions to weddings—and offer event design services for seamless execution.

Event design services

DOVA [dohva] — is a portmanteau for Don Vale — the 19th century village that grew up to become modern-day Cabbagetown. We hope to become as much a part of the area’s history as the original hamlet on which it stands.

Our chef & creative director

Chef Roberto Marotta met Jacqueline Nicosia in his hometown of Milazzo, a port city on the northeast coast of Sicily. He was a chef at his family’s restaurant. She was a student finishing her studies abroad. They came back to Canada to start a family and their own business.

Their first restaurant, ARDO, was named after their son—who had a particularly difficult time pronouncing his full name, Leonardo. After the birth of their daughter Vivienne, they launched Vivi Imports to bring the finest ingredients from Sicily’s shores to Canada. And now they complete their triple crown with DOVA.

Roberto

Roberto grew up seeing his mother prepare classic Sicilian dishes for family and friends. He honed his skills by cooking for others before embarking on formal training in his late teens. He’s become seasoned in kitchens at various establishments from the Mediterranean to North America. And now his trademark cooking style and reverence for the art of Sicilian cuisine adds vibrancy to Toronto’s restaurant scene.

Jacqueline

Jacqueline has spent the better part of the past decade designing events and enhancing experiences for corporate clients—both as a private consultant and as partner at ARDO and Vivi. Her aesthetic finesse and inherent love of hospitality continue to impress patrons at each location.
