Main floor

Our main floor dining room accommodates 40 for seated meals and 60 for standing receptions. While it’s clean and modern, it has a timeless atmosphere that’s brightened by daylight and candle-lit in the evenings.

Cellar

Our cellar (20 seated/35 standing) is dim and intimate with dramatic tapestries and floor tiling.

Patio

Our white-washed back patio feels like an out-of-town getaway. It perfectly entertains 48 for full dinner service and 70 guests for cocktails.
